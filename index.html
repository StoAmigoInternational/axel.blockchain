        Middlemen OWN Digital Content

        Distributors completely control paid content. They select what type of files can be sold. They choose where people can buy them. They determine how much they're sold for. Everything.

        Control has been ripped away from content creators by these "middleman" corporations. And there have been no real alternatives. Until now.

        The Cutting-Edge P2P Content Marketplace Giving Control Back To Creators

        The AXEL Marketplace will let people manage each and every aspect of how their files are sold. It's a selling process so easy that anyone can become an entrepreneur in minutes, even with no experience. Setup is free and transaction fees practically non-existent. No arbitrary rules. No spying.

        It'll be THE destination to sell and buy anything digital.

        Built on our AXEL app with millions of current users, the AXEL Marketplace will utilize unique patented technology to enable direct, peer-to-peer file exchanges between buyers and sellers.

        Selling online today is expensive. Starting with AXEL will be free. No storefront fees: anyone can instantly start selling without spending a dime. Transaction fees will be small and transparent.

        Global decentralized selling

        A decentralized marketplace means users keep and own their content. Patented technology ensures everything is directly from device to device. No arbitrary censorship or regulations.

        FAQ

        • Explain this to me in plain English.
          1. We have a multi-platform file sharing and transfer app that tons of people use. It's called AXEL. It's neat.
          2. Content creators using our app will be able to easily sell their files straight from their devices through PayPal with a few clicks, without startup costs. We call this function AXEL Pay.
          3. Further, we plan to let anyone using AXEL Pay post their files on a global decentralized marketplace, allowing anyone to monetize their content quickly and easily. They will keep up to 95% of every sale.
        • What does it cost to sell on the AXEL Marketplace?

          There is a planned 2% platform fee to be charged for creators selling on the Marketplace. An additional 3% transaction fee will go to PayPal.

          The platform fee is primarily so we can maintain and improve the AXEL Marketplace, making it a better destination to buy and sell.

          And to keep this in perspective: comparable services charge 30% - 90%.

          2. We have a live app that you can use today, across multiple devices. Windows, Mac, Android, iOS, and web. It works, and it's had millions of downloads with ratings above 4.4/5. We're not starting from scratch.
          3. We have patented technology that no one else can replicate. That means the way we're doing things cannot be "copied" by anyone else. Not some newfangled startup, not even large corporations. It's unique and only we can do it.
